Pikachu changes

  • In Generation 1, Pikachu has a base Special stat of 50.
  • In Generations 1-5, Pikachu has a base Defense of 30.
  • In Generations 2-5, Pikachu has a base Special Defense of 40.
  • In Generations 1-4, Pikachu has a base experience yield of 82.
  • In Generation 5, Pikachu has a base experience yield of 105.
  • In Pokémon Yellow, Pikachu has a base Friendship value of 90.
  • In Generations 2-7, Pikachu has a base Friendship value of 70.
